\section{Conjugate Distribution} 

From Bayes theorem
  \begin{align}
    p(\theta | x) = \frac{p(x|\theta)p(\theta)}{\int p(x|\theta')p(\theta')d\theta'}
  \end{align}
All membets of the exponential family have conjugate priors.
